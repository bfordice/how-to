How to build a wooden chair

This is a hand build all wooden chair. The seat is made from inch thick plywood, and the legs and supports are made from any material desired.

  • Step 1: Cut the seat to desired shape
  • Step 2: Cut legs to correct length and width
  • Step 3: Cut supports to correct length
  • Step 4: Secure supports to the legs with 2 inch pocket screws
  • Step 5: Sand bottom half and top half
  • Step 6: Construct the back support
  • Step 7: Paint the chair
  • Step 8: Lightly sand top and bottom
