How to build a coffee table

This is a hand build all wooden coffee table. The tabletop is made from pine, and the legs and supports are made from oak. The finish is a dark walnut finish sanded with 800 grit sandpaper.

  • Step 1: Cut Table Top to desired length
  • Step 2: Cut 4x4 legs to correct length
  • Step 3: Cut 2x4 supports to correct length
  • Step 4: Secure supports to the legs with 2.5 inch pocket screws
  • Step 5: Sand bottom half and top half
  • Step 6: Stain the table top with desire stain
  • Step 7: Paint bottom half
  • Step 8: Secure Bottm half to top with 2 inch screws
  • Step 9: Lightly sand top and bottom
